VisionBridge: Empowering the Future of Inclusive ICT Education

An AI-Driven Multimodal Learning Ecosystem for Visually Impaired Students in Sri Lanka.

VisionBridge is a pioneering research initiative dedicated to breaking the barriers faced by visually impaired students in mastering ICT. By integrating advanced Edge-based Gesture Struggle Detection and AI-powered Diagram Interpretation, we provide a seamless, independent learning experience. Our mission is to ensure that no student is left behind due to visual challenges, especially in mastering complex logical concepts of the G.C.E. O/L ICT syllabus.

Project Overview

📚 Research Focus

Addressing the accessibility barriers in ICT education for visually impaired (VI) students in Sri Lanka. We focus on bridging the gap between standard curricula and inclusive digital learning tools through multimodal interaction.

🎯 Key Objectives

To develop an edge-based gesture struggle detection model, an AI-driven diagram interpreter, and a haptic-audio feedback system to facilitate independent learning for Grade 10 and 11 VI students.

✅ Deliverables

A functional mobile learning ecosystem, comprehensive research findings on low-latency struggle detection, and technical publications validated through testing with the Employers’ Federation of Ceylon (EFC).

Research Highlights

Edge-based Struggle Detection

Our core novelty lies in an offline-ready Random Forest Classifier (RFC) implemented in JavaScript. It identifies navigational struggles in real-time with 85% accuracy and provides adaptive, simplified audio instructions.

Multimodal Innovation

VisionBridge leverages a combination of custom gestures, haptic (vibration) feedback, and high-quality audio cues to create a spatial mental map for users navigating complex ICT lessons.

Predictive Behavioral Analytics

By utilizing XGBoost (F1-Macro: 0.935), we analyze student interaction logs to compute a Behavioral Learning Difficulty Index (BLDI), providing teachers with data-driven insights into student performance.

Inclusive Diagram Interpretation

Leveraging the Gemini Vision API, we transform visually complex logic gates and truth tables into descriptive auditory formats, making abstract ICT concepts accessible for the first time.
